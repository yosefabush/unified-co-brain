CloudVault AI Security & Compliance Overview (Public)

1. Encryption Standards
All customer data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Data in transit is secured via TLS 1.3.

2. Compliance
We are fully SOC2 Type II compliant and GDPR ready. We conduct annual penetration testing.

3. Data Export & Portability
CloudVault believes in data freedom. Our platform supports comprehensive data export capabilities, allowing users to download their full dataset in all major standard formats at any time.

4. Scalability
Our infrastructure is built on a serverless architecture designed to handle unlimited concurrent requests without performance degradation.
